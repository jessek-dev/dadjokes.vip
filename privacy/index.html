Overview

Dad Jokes ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • None required: Dad Jokes does not require account creation or personal information to function

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, joke views, ratings you provide, and refresh counts
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have a free or premium subscription (managed through App Store/Google Play)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names or email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files
  • Browsing history

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver the daily featured joke
  • Track your daily refresh count (3 for free users, 10 for premium)
  • Save your joke ratings locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Manage premium subscriptions through your app store

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your joke ratings and preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase to store and retrieve jokes. Only anonymous usage analytics are collected
  • No Personal Data on Servers: We do not store any personally identifiable information on our servers

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

  • Used for joke storage and anonymous analytics
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Google AdMob (Free version only)

  • Displays advertisements to free users
  • May collect advertising identifiers
  • Subject to Google's Ad Policies

App Store / Google Play

  • Manages subscriptions and payments
  • We do not have access to your payment information

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Ratings: Clear your joke ratings by deleting and reinstalling the app
  • Ads: Upgrade to Premium to remove all advertisements
  • Analytics: You can opt out of analytics collection in your device settings

Children's Privacy

Dad Jokes does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains humor suitable for all ages.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. Information is only shared:

  • With service providers necessary for app functionality (Firebase)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or user safety

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or our website. Continued use of Dad Jokes after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
