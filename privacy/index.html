Overview

Dad Jokes ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • None required: Dad Jokes does not require account creation or personal information to function

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, joke views, ratings you provide, and refresh counts
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have a free or premium subscription (managed through App Store/Google Play)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names or email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files
  • Browsing history

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver the daily featured joke
  • Track your daily refresh count (3 for free users, 10 for premium)
  • Save your joke ratings locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Manage premium subscriptions through your app store

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your joke ratings and preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase to store and retrieve jokes. Only anonymous usage analytics are collected
  • No Personal Data on Servers: We do not store any personally identifiable information on our servers

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

  • Used for joke storage and anonymous analytics
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Google AdMob (Free version only)

  • Displays advertisements to free users
  • May collect advertising identifiers
  • Subject to Google's Ad Policies

App Store / Google Play

  • Manages subscriptions and payments
  • We do not have access to your payment information

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Ratings: Clear your joke ratings by deleting and reinstalling the app
  • Ads: Upgrade to Premium to remove all advertisements
  • Analytics: You can opt out of analytics collection in your device settings

Children's Privacy

Dad Jokes does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains humor suitable for all ages.

Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

If you created an account using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  1. Open the Dad Jokes app on your device
  2. Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top navigation bar
  3. Select "My Account" from the menu
  4. Tap "Delete Account" at the bottom of the screen
  5. Type "DELETE MY ACCOUNT" in the confirmation dialog
  6. Confirm by tapping "Yes, Delete Account"

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account Profile: Your account information, including name, email, and profile photo
  • Saved Jokes: All jokes you've saved to your personal "Vault"
  • Personalized Content: Any personalized jokes created using your name
  • Preferences: All app settings and notification preferences
  • Usage History: Your viewing history and joke interaction data
  • Authentication: Your Google or Apple Sign-In connection to Dad Jokes

Deletion Timeframe

Account deletion is immediate and irreversible:

  • Your account and all data are deleted from our servers within seconds of confirmation
  • You will be automatically signed out of the app
  • Your data cannot be recovered after deletion
  • You can create a new account at any time, but previous data will not be restored

Anonymous Users

If you use Dad Jokes without creating an account (anonymous mode):

  • No server-side data deletion is needed, as we don't store personal data for anonymous users
  • To clear local data (ratings and preferences), simply delete and reinstall the app
  • Anonymous usage data may persist in analytics for up to 90 days per Firebase retention policies

Post-Deletion Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • No personal data is retained on our servers
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained for app improvement
  • Any user-submitted jokes you contributed may remain in the app (attributed as "Community Member" instead of your name)
  • Subscription records managed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store are subject to their respective policies

Questions About Account Deletion

If you encounter issues deleting your account or have questions about data removal, please contact us at privacy@dadjokes.vip. We will respond within 48 hours.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. Information is only shared:

  • With service providers necessary for app functionality (Firebase)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or user safety

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or our website. Continued use of Dad Jokes after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
