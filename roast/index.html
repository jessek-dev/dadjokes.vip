The Art of the Perfect Name Roast

There's something uniquely hilarious about a roast that uses someone's actual name. It's personal, it's unexpected, and it hits different than a generic burn. The best friend roasts walk the line between savage and silly — they make the whole group laugh, including the person getting roasted.

Roasting has deep roots in comedy. From the Friars Club celebrity roasts of the 1950s to Comedy Central specials, the format works because it's built on affection. You only roast people you actually like. When you add someone's name into the mix, it transforms a general joke into a personalized experience that feels tailor-made for your friend group.

Name-based roasts are funnier because they catch people off guard. When someone hears "Hey Jessica..." they expect a compliment — not a perfectly crafted burn. That surprise factor is what makes personalized roasts the most shared content among friend groups. Our AI generates roasts that play on name meanings, pop culture references, and classic roast formats.
