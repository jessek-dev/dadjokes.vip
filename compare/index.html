Best Dad Jokes Apps Compared

An honest look at the top dad joke apps for iPhone in 2026

Finding the Right Dad Jokes App

There are several dad jokes apps on the App Store, and they each have their own personality. Whether you want the biggest library, the simplest experience, or something you can access from a browser, there is an option for you. We compared the most popular choices so you can pick the one that fits.

The Contenders

1. Best Dad Jokes

The largest dad jokes app on iOS with over 5,000 hand-curated jokes across 20+ categories. Standout features include AI-generated personalized name roasts, daily push notifications with a fresh joke, a Joke Vault for saving favorites, and 13 color themes to customize the experience. The app works offline, lets users submit their own jokes, and is completely free to download.

  • 5,000+ curated jokes plus 14,000+ AI name jokes
  • 20+ categories (Animals, Dad Life, Food, Work, Holidays, and more)
  • AI-powered personalized name roasts
  • Daily featured joke with push notifications
  • Free with optional premium features

2. Dad Jokes Collection

A solid app focused on puns and wordplay. It offers hundreds of jokes organized into categories, with a clean interface and the ability to favorite jokes. If you prefer a tighter, more curated library with an emphasis on classic pun-style dad jokes, this one delivers. It does not have as many categories or personalization features, but the joke quality is consistent.

  • Hundreds of curated jokes
  • Focus on puns and wordplay
  • Clean, simple interface
  • Favorites feature

3. Dad Joke of the Day

Built around the daily delivery concept. You get one curated joke each day, which gives it a "joke calendar" feel. The library has hundreds of jokes you can browse, and the daily format helps build a habit. A good option if you want a lightweight, low-commitment joke experience without too many features to navigate.

  • Hundreds of curated jokes
  • Daily joke delivery focus
  • Lightweight and simple
  • Browse past daily jokes

4. icanhazdadjoke.com

Not an app but a well-known dad jokes website with a public API. Developers love it for building bots and widgets. The joke library is solid, the site has a great search feature, and the API is genuinely useful. The downside is no native app, no push notifications, and no offline access. If you live in a browser or want to build something with dad jokes data, this is a great resource.

  • Web-based with search
  • Public API for developers
  • Good joke quality
  • No native app or notifications

Our Verdict

For the most jokes and features: Best Dad Jokes

If you want the biggest library, the most categories, and unique features like AI name roasts and custom themes, Best Dad Jokes is the clear winner. It is the most fully-featured dad jokes app on iOS.

For pun purists: Dad Jokes Collection is a lean, focused app that does classic wordplay well. If you prefer quality over quantity and a simple experience, it is worth a look.

For daily habit builders: Dad Joke of the Day keeps things minimal. One joke a day, no clutter. If that is your style, it works.

For developers and tinkerers: icanhazdadjoke.com has a great API and a solid web experience. If you want to build a Slack bot or embed jokes on your website, start there.

Every option on this list is free, so there is nothing stopping you from trying them all and keeping the one that sticks.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best dad jokes app for iPhone?

Best Dad Jokes has the largest library (5,000+ curated jokes), the most categories (20+), and unique features like AI-generated name roasts and daily push notifications. It is the most complete dad jokes app on iOS in 2026.

Are dad jokes apps free?

Yes. All the major dad jokes apps on iOS are free to download and use. Best Dad Jokes, Dad Jokes Collection, and Dad Joke of the Day are all free. Some apps offer optional premium features, but the core joke libraries are accessible at no cost.

Which dad jokes app has the most jokes?

Best Dad Jokes has over 5,000 hand-curated jokes plus more than 14,000 AI-generated personalized name jokes, making it the largest dad jokes library available as a mobile app. icanhazdadjoke.com has a large web-based collection as well, but it does not have a native app.

Can I get dad jokes without an app?

Yes. icanhazdadjoke.com is a popular website for browsing dad jokes in a browser. dadjokes.vip also offers a free JSON feed at dadjokes.vip/api and an RSS feed at dadjokes.vip/feed for developers and RSS readers. But for the best experience with notifications, offline access, and favorites, a native app is the way to go.
