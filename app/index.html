Frequently Asked Questions

How many jokes are in the Dad Jokes app?

We have 19,139 jokes and counting! Our collection includes 2,396 classic dad jokes and 16,743 AI-generated personalized name jokes across 20+ categories including animals, food, work & money, seasonal, and more.

Is the Dad Jokes app free?

Yes! Dad Jokes is completely free to download and use on iOS. You get access to all 19,139+ jokes, daily featured jokes, and the ability to save your favorites. We also offer premium features like AI-generated personalized name jokes, custom color themes, and an ad-free experience.

What are dad jokes?

Dad jokes are wholesome, family-friendly jokes known for their puns, wordplay, and groan-worthy humor. They're the kind of jokes dads tell that make everyone roll their eyes but secretly smile. Think clean humor with clever punchlines that anyone can enjoy.

What platforms is the Dad Jokes app available on?

Dad Jokes is currently available on iOS (iPhone and iPad). You can download it for free from the Apple App Store. An Android version is planned for the future.

Can I submit my own dad jokes to the app?

Yes! We welcome user-submitted jokes through the app. Simply tap the submit button in the app to share your best dad jokes with the community. All submissions are reviewed before being added to ensure they meet our family-friendly standards.

What categories of jokes are available?

We have 20+ joke categories including: Animals, Around the House, Clothing, Dad Life, Family, Food, Holidays, Music, Nature, Puns, Science, Seasonal, Sports, Technology, Travel, Wife, Work & Money, and more. Each category is curated with the best dad jokes in that theme.

Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

The app works both online and offline! We cache thousands of jokes locally so you can enjoy dad jokes even without an internet connection. Daily featured jokes and AI-generated personalized jokes require internet access.

What are AI-generated name jokes?

AI-generated name jokes are personalized jokes about your name created using artificial intelligence. Enter any name and get custom jokes that playfully roast or celebrate that person. It's a unique feature that makes sharing jokes even more fun and personal.
