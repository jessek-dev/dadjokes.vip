Frequently Asked Questions

How do I refresh my daily joke?
Free users get 3 daily refreshes, while Premium users get 10. Simply tap the "Get me another!" button on the home screen. Your refresh count resets every day at midnight.
What's included in Premium?
Premium subscribers enjoy:
  • More featured daily jokes
  • Access to exclusive premium jokes
  • Ad-free experience
  • Early access to new features
  • Our heartfelt thanks for supporting us!
How do I share a joke?
Tap the share icon at the top right of any joke card. You can share via text, social media, or save the joke as an image.
Can I submit my own dad jokes?
Yes please! We're always looking for new groan-worthy content! Send us your best/worst dad jokes using the contact form below.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Why on earth would you do that?? Well, if you must... manage your subscription through your device's app store:
  • iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions
