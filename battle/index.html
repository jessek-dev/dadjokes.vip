    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does Dad Joke Battle work?

    Two random dad jokes face off head-to-head. Read both jokes and tap the one you think is funnier. After voting, you will see the vote percentages from your own battle history. Keep battling to build up your personal leaderboard of the funniest dad jokes!

    Are the vote results saved?

    Your votes are stored locally on your device using your browser's storage. This means your battle history and leaderboard are personal to you. There is no global leaderboard yet, but every vote helps you discover your own favorite dad jokes.

    How many jokes are in the battle pool?

    The battle draws from thousands of curated dad jokes. For the full collection of 19,000+ jokes across 20+ categories, download the free Dad Jokes app. You can rate jokes there too!
